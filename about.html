Rise of New Brand Vehicle in Bhutan

The rise of new vehicles in Bhutan, particularly electric vehicles (EVs), is closely linked to the country's commitment to sustainability and its unique environmental goals. Bhutan, known for its philosophy of Gross National Happiness (GNH) and its environmental focus, has been gradually embracing modern and eco-friendly transportation technologies.
